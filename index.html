Why a Friction Intelligence Platform

Your golden path is only golden if developers actually take it.

Most platform teams ship an IDP, then fly blind. Dashboards tell you what was deployed but not where developers struggled, bypassed the paved road, or quietly gave up. Friction intelligence closes that gap: it measures the real experience of using your platform, so you can fix what slows people down before it turns into shadow tooling, ticket backlogs, and stalled adoption.

Psychology

Measure behavior. Never blame people.

Friction is a signal, not a verdict. When an engineer bypasses the golden path, it usually means the safe path was slower, harder, or unclear — the platform failed them, not the other way around. Healert is built so that data drives better paving, not surveillance.
